Sanchraka Udawa 2024

The Sri Lanka Association of Inbound Tour Operators (SLAITO) is hosting the 11th edition of 'Sancharaka Udawa 2024', a tourism products meet mart on May 17th and 18th, 2024, at the Sirimavo Bandaranaike Exhibition Halls at BMICH.

The event aims to connect lesser-known entrepreneurs in the Tourism Industry with mainstream stakeholders. It provides a platform for small and medium tourism service providers from various regions to showcase their offerings, network with tour operators, and tap into wider industry opportunities.
