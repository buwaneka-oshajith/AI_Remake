Our company was founded by a team of professionals with extensive experience and broad exposure to Information Technology. Our team comprises young, qualified business graduates and engineers from prestigious universities around the world. Specializing in all aspects of Information Technology since 2019, A.I TECH has positioned itself as a leader in providing ICT Solutions in the Sri Lankan market. We deliver solutions that add value, provide benefits, and address diverse and complex requirements across various vertical markets. At A.I TECH, we drive business transformation for our clients through innovative technology utilization. We bring together top talents and cutting-edge technologies from around the globe to offer creative, intelligent solutions to any business challenge. With a proven track record of excellence in services ranging from personal computers to business consulting, system integration, and IT and business process outsourcing, we ensure an exceptional customer experience. Leveraging the best of Sri Lankan technical and business expertise, along with leading global technologies including Dell and Intel, A.I TECH caters to all sectors of the Sri Lankan market. Our satisfied customer base includes small and medium businesses, corporate enterprises, and government entities.
